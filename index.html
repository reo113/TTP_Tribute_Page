So gather up your itty-bitty courage and skedaddle out of my beloved domain. This ain't no place for fancy-pants city folk or curious wanderers. I've got mud to wallow in, bugs to chat with, and onions to peel! You think you can just waltz into my swamp, disturbing the peace and tranquility? Well, think again! This ain't no tourist attraction or picnic spot. It's a sanctuary for ogres like me, and I don't take kindly to uninvited guests. Now, grab your map, retrace your steps, and march yourself outta here. Don't even think about leaving a trace behind, 'cause I got a keen nose for intruders. And trust me, you won't like what happens if I catch you lingering. So, don't test my patience, little human. The swamp is off-limits to the likes of you. The sooner you're outta here, the better for both of us. And don't even think about coming back unless you bring some donkey-approved snacks! Now scram! And don't let the door hit ya on the way out... if there was a door, which there ain't 'cause this is my swamp!"
